Arjun's Ventures.

Here are my social projects. They range from events I organise to changes I try to make in my community.

Revitalizing ENCMP

Winter 2017
At the UofA Faculty of Engineering, the first year programming course is an introduction for most. Brittany and I are working with Ivan Fair and Dileepan Joseph to make this introduction as possitive as possible. Dr. Joseph especially is making great strides and I'll be helping him gather feedback and make decisions on how the course can be improved!

HackTime

Winter 2017
HackTime seeks to bring about a hackathon-like experience for coders around the University. The goal is to encourage students to work on side projects by creating a safe space and community. This initiative begins in the Fall of 2017.

HackEd 2017

Fall 2016
Making events bigger and better might be more difficult than doing them the first time. This year we sported more hackers, more sponsors, more mentors, more judges and we still accomplished the goal of inspiring and growing passion. It reminds me why I do it all.

CE approval of AU Courses

Fall 2016
Motivated by my unwillingness to take uninteresting courses, I asked the Director of Computer Engineering to approve two new courses for credit. I had selected courses the University did not offer already, so he approved them for me and made them available for everyone. :D

How to CompE workshops

Fall 2016
Another initiative from Brittany Lamorie and I, four workshops covering job-related topics (Networking, Resumes, Job Search and Interviews) specifically for Computer Engineers. Read my emotionally charged blog post for more background, but the workshops were great and hopefully will improve next year!

ECE Week, HackEd 2016

Fall 2015
It is CompE Club VPX's role to organize and run ECE Week and HackEd. I created websites, created a professional sponsorship package, contacted over 100 different companies, interacted with industry professionals, and MC-ed the hackathon's opening ceremonies. Overall ECE Week and the Hackathon were successful events!
